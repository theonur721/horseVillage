Located on 98 acres of rolling countryside in Melbourne Australia, the "Honour Horse Village" offers a wide range of horses, riding training and healthcare services. Our vibrant and active community sets a new standard for family-friendly vacations with exceptional wellness programs, delicious cuisine and excellent amenities.

Honour Horse Village is the perfect size; It is small enough to build meaningful relationships and large enough to offer a wide range of activities and services. We cultivate the atmosphere of casual elegance, welcoming warmth and unparalleled hospitality found only here. Come see for yourself!

ABOUT US ?

I was inspired by living among friends;

White Horse Village is an active community of doers, thinkers, doers, artists and lifelong learners. Without the demands of house maintenance or yard work, residents are free to explore all of life's limitless possibilities. Together we build meaningful relationships, develop new ideas, get involved, and feel part of our collective.

When you visit us you will be greeted with a warm hello and friendly conversation. Our new residents often tell us how quickly they connect with the people who live here and form lasting friendships.

leading the way;

In addition to being a horse farm, White Horse Village is an active senior living community offering 331 independent housing options and a two-story Wellness Center with 55 private care rooms, 50 personal care rooms and 30 private rooms in the memory support neighborhood. We are progressive in our thoughts and actions and passionate about leading the way in our innovative and sustainable approach to living.

As a nonprofit organization, White Horse Village reinvests profits into the organization to further our mission and vision.
